Characters of Whispers of Ink

Adnan Mirza

Protagonist

A quiet writer haunted by unfinished letters and memories that bleed into his present life.

Zara Iqbal

Supporting Character

A journalist who believes every silence hides a truth waiting to be uncovered.

Hassan Rafiq

Antagonist

A man whose past mistakes echo through the lives of everyone around him.

Amara Khan

Childhood friend

Adnan’s playful and intuitive childhood friend. She often helps uncover secrets and lightens tense moments with her charm.

Omar Qureshi

Mentor

Adnan’s wise mentor. A grounding presence, offering guidance and perspective during difficult times.

Nadia Sameer

Colleague

Zara’s ambitious colleague. Clever and determined, she occasionally challenges the protagonists’ ideas and adds tension to the story.
