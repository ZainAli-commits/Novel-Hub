Characters of Echoes of Midnight

Rayan Siddiq

Protagonist

The main character, thoughtful and mysterious, navigating through secrets and shadows of his past.

Layla Ahmed

Confidante

Rayan’s trusted friend, intelligent and curious, who helps uncover hidden truths in the story.

Danish Malik

Antagonist

A cunning and intense character who challenges Rayan at every step, adding tension and suspense.

Sara Fayyaz

Journalist

Determined and professional, Sara investigates events around Rayan, pushing the plot forward with her insight.

Owais Khan

Mentor

A wise and calm mentor figure, offering guidance and perspective to Rayan and others during critical moments.

Naveen Arif

Minor Antagonist

Ambitious and sly, Naveen creates tension and obstacles, challenging the protagonists’ goals in subtle ways.
